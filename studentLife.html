Academic Activities

ACADEMIC ACTIVITIES

The College has adequate classrooms and lecture halls for students filled with chairs and writing platforms. The College has four compartment science laboratories for training and because all our courses are practical based there are demonstration/practical rooms and demonstration ground to enhance learning skills. The College has a computer laboratory where all new entrants are acquainted with computer operation.

Social Amenities

Accommodation:

The College is non-residential by Government’s policy but houses are available at reasonable prices around the College.

Medical Service:

The College has a clinic, which is well equipped with modern medical facilities and serves the dual purpose of; serving the academic community and the surrounding Imelu area and, as internal practice area for all students.

Establishment of Tertiary Institution Social Health, Insurance Programme (TISHIP) and Student Welfare Insurance Scheme (SWIS) have also been implemented with relevant and appropriate authority. The State Government recently established the State Primary Health Care Development Board and the Osun State Health Insurance Scheme, both of which will improve health care delivery and training both in the College and the State at large.

Sport:

Our students take part in intra and inter-college competitions in and outside Osun State. The College has a functional Football Field, Volleyball Pitch, Handball Pitch, Table Tennis Board and ‘Ayo Olopon’.

Transportation:

The College has students bus for conveyance of students to practice areas. However, individuals can make use of commercial transport facilities. The Community Health Department and General Health Studies Department also have departmental students’ buses bought by the Students Associations.

Club and Societies:

The College has a Students Union body of which every student is a member. Other societies and clubs approved by the College are also available alongside Departmental Associations where students join to promote inter-personal relationships and harmonious College environment. CULTISM IS STRICTLY PROHIBITED.

Religion Activities

RELIGION ACTIVITIES

The College has a mosque for daily Muslim prayers and Friday Jumat Service. There is also an interdenominational chapel for Christian worship.
