General Objective

The institution believes in equal distribution of health resources, as people everywhere have common basic needs for existence. The College objectives include training of competent health care professionals to meet the ever changing health needs of individuals and community, with a view to attaining a level of health that will make an individual productive and useful to himself, his family, community and the society at large.

Vision Statement

To be a health training institution par excellence, producing competent and efficient graduates, providing modern Primary Health Care services

Mission

  1. To provide courses of study, training and research in health technology leading to attainment of Certificates, Professional Diplomas, and National and Higher National Diplomas.
  2. To provide avenue for continuing education in health and health care related fields.
  3. To provide avenue for re-training of health workers.
  4. To train middle level man power in the health sector.
